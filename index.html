What services does Techpaltan specialize in?

Techpaltan specializes in custom Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) development, high-performance web applications, dynamic corporate websites, and cross-platform mobile app development tailored for business growth.

Can you customize an ERP system for my specific industry?

Absolutely. We build fully customizable ERP solutions designed to match your specific workflows, whether you are in manufacturing, retail, healthcare, logistics, or finance. We map our software to your exact operational needs rather than forcing you to adapt to rigid templates.

Do you build mobile apps for both iOS and Android?

Yes, we develop both native and cross-platform mobile applications. We utilize modern frameworks to ensure a seamless, intuitive, and high-performing user experience across all Apple and Android devices simultaneously.

How long does it take to develop a custom software solution?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A standard corporate website might take 4-8 weeks, while a comprehensive enterprise ERP system can take anywhere from 3 to 6+ months. We provide clear development roadmaps during the initial consultation phase.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes, we believe digital transformation is an ongoing process. We offer dedicated post-launch support, continuous server maintenance, security updates, and feature enhancements to ensure your applications scale securely as your business grows.

Why choose Techpaltan as your digital partner?

We combine robust technical expertise with a deep understanding of business strategy. Our agile development methodology, commitment to data security, transparent communication, and focus on generating measurable ROI make us a trusted partner for your long-term digital success.
